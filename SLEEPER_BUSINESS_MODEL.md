- **Paid Leagues**: Take small % of entry fees
- **Cash Prizes**: Processing fees on payouts
- **Note**: Many platforms use this model (DraftKings, FanDuel)

### 5. **Data Licensing**
- **User Behavior Data**: Aggregated, anonymized insights
- **Market Research**: Sell insights to brands/agencies
- **Revenue**: B2B contracts

### 6. **White Label Solutions**
- **Enterprise**: License platform to companies
- **Custom Leagues**: For businesses/organizations
- **Revenue**: Licensing fees

### **Network Effects**
- Free = More users = More valuable platform
- More users = Better matchmaking = Better experience
- Better experience = More engagement = More ad revenue

### **Freemium Strategy**
- Free core product builds trust
- Premium features for power users (5-10% convert)
- Most users stay free, but engaged users pay

- With 1M+ users, even small ad revenue adds up
- $1-2 per user per year from ads = $1-2M revenue
- Premium conversion of 5% = additional revenue
