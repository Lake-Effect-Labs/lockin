## Step 1: Add Build to Testing Group

### Option A: Use Existing "TE Team (Expo)" Group

1. In TestFlight, click **"INTERNAL TESTING"** (left sidebar)
2. Click **"TE Team (Expo)"** group
3. Scroll to **"Builds"** section
4. Click **"+"** button (top right of Builds section)
5. Select **"1.0.0 (1)"** from the list
6. Click **"Add"**

### Option B: Create New "Friends & Family" Group

1. In TestFlight, click **"INTERNAL TESTING"** (left sidebar)
2. Click **"+"** button (top right)
3. Click **"Create Group"**
4. Name: **"Friends & Family"**
5. Click **"Create"**
6. Follow steps 3-6 from Option A above

---

## Step 2: Add Friend Email Addresses

1. Still in your testing group (TE Team or Friends & Family)
2. Scroll to **"Testers"** section
3. Click **"+"** button next to **"Testers"**

### Enter Emails

**Option 1: One per line**
```
friend1@email.com
friend2@email.com
friend3@email.com
```

**Option 2: Comma-separated**
```
friend1@email.com, friend2@email.com, friend3@email.com
```

4. Click **"Add"**

## Troubleshooting

### Friends Don't Get Email

**Check:**
- Email addresses are correct
- Check spam folder
- Wait 5-10 minutes
- Manually send invite:
  - Click **"Invite"** button next to tester name
  - Or select multiple â†’ **"Invite All"**

### Build Not Showing in Group

**Check:**
- Build status is "Ready to Submit" (not "Processing")
- You added the correct build (1.0.0 (1))
- Refresh the page

### Friends Can't Install

**Check:**
- They have TestFlight app installed
- They accepted email invite
- iOS version (needs iOS 13+)
- Build is in their group
